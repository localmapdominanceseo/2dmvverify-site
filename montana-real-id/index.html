To get a Montana REAL ID in 2026, you must visit a Montana MVD office in person with your identity, Social Security, and residency documents. The cost is $40 — same as a standard renewal. This guide covers every step.

A Montana REAL ID is a federally compliant driver's license marked with a star symbol, issued by Montana MVD. It is required to board domestic U.S. flights and enter certain federal buildings. If you carry a valid U.S. passport, you do not need a REAL ID — a standard license is sufficient for driving.

Do You Need a Montana REAL ID?

You need a REAL ID (or a valid passport) for two purposes: boarding domestic U.S. flights and entering certain federal facilities. For everyday driving in Montana, a standard license is valid. Check your current license for a star symbol — if it has one, you already have a REAL ID.

How to Get a Montana REAL ID: Step-by-Step

Gather your four document categories

Collect your identity document, SSN proof, two Montana residency proofs, and any name-change documents. Confirm the current accepted list on the Montana MVD website.

Schedule a Montana MVD appointment

REAL ID requires an in-person visit. Book an appointment to reduce wait time. Bring original documents only.

Complete your application in person

Staff review and scan your documents. You will have your photo taken. Your originals are returned to you.

Pay the Montana REAL ID fee

The cost is $40 — the standard Montana license renewal fee. Pay directly to Montana MVD at the office.

Receive your REAL ID by mail

You get a temporary document at the office. Your REAL ID-compliant license arrives by mail in 1–3 weeks with a star in the upper corner.

Frequently Asked Questions: Montana REAL ID

What is a REAL ID and why do I need one in Montana?
A REAL ID is a federally compliant license marked with a star. You need it to board domestic U.S. flights and enter certain federal facilities. Your standard Montana license remains valid for driving — REAL ID is only required for air travel and federal access without a passport.
How much does a Montana REAL ID cost in 2026?
The Montana REAL ID costs $40 — the same as the standard driver's license renewal fee. There is no additional REAL ID surcharge in Montana.
Can I get a Montana REAL ID online?
No. Federal law requires all first-time REAL ID applicants to appear in person at a Montana MVD office with original documents. This applies in Montana and every other state.
Does my current Montana license qualify as a REAL ID?
Check your license for a gold or black star in the upper corner. If it has one, it is REAL ID-compliant. If not, you can upgrade at your next renewal by visiting a Montana MVD office with the required documents.
